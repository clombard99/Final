THE JUNIOR WONDERS STAFF

Mary O'Connell, the Founder and Director of Junior Wonders Nursery School, is a graduate of Hunter College and holds a Masters Degree in Elementary Education. She was a 26 year veteran elementary school teacher when she founded the Playful Wonders After School Program in 1986. Having raised seven of her own children and as a working mother she realized the need for quality childcare in the area. In 1992 she extended the program to include daytime classes for pre-school aged children and the Junior Wonders Nursery School was born.

 

The Junior Wonders and Playful Wonders staff of dedicated professionals insure the best possible environment for all of our children. All of our staff members attend yearly classes and workshops in early childhood education, safety and first aid. We strive to ensure that your child's first school experience is the happiest, safest and best it can be.

Parents interested in more information should call (917) 617 0903 or email us. We'd love to hear from you!
