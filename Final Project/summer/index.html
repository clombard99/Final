Junior Wonders June Enrichment 2017

– June 5th-23rd –

This June our new multicultural theme is centers around globetrotting across four continents with stops in France, Kenya, China, Brazil, and then back home to the U.S. 

Designed to bridge the gap between school and this special program offers an educational theme and a range of other exciting and fun activities including:

• Indoor and outdoor play
• Dance & Creative Movement with Independent, Susan Maysonet 
• Sing along with Zev
• Local Presentations by Community Members, representing each country, will include cultural music, storytelling, food samplings, costumes, and other activities. 

Program Info:

The program will be held at Junior Wonders and classes will meet from 9am to Noon, Mon-Fri from June 5th - June 23rd. We will offer a Fun With Twos & Threes class and two Pre-K classes.

• Fun With 2s & 3s: 15 sessions - $545* 

• Pre-K class: 15 sessions - $605*

*A 5% discount will be applied to completed applications submitted by Friday, April 21st
Full payment is due byFriday, May 12th. A $100 deposit is due with the application.

Space for the program is limited, so reserve a spot for your child today. For more info contact Hope O’Connell at (917) 617 0903, visit the school office or email us. We'd love to hear from you!
